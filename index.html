About Rolex

Rolex is a Swiss luxury watchmaker renowned for its precision, craftsmanship, and timeless design. Founded in 1905, Rolex has become synonymous with high-quality timepieces and is a symbol of status and achievement. Our watches are designed with the finest materials and the latest technology to ensure durability and precision.

  • Founded: 1905
  • Headquarters: Geneva, Switzerland
  • Specialty: Luxury watches
  • Notable Models: Submariner, Daytona, Datejust
